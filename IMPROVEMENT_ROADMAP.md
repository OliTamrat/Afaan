### 4.1 Remove Chat Restrictions
**Current:** Limited responses, boundaries on topics
**Target:** Free-flowing conversation with AI tutor

**Improvements:**
- Remove topic restrictions (allow discussion of any subject)
- Increase context window (remember longer conversations)
- Add conversation modes:
  - **Practice Mode:** Focus on language learning
  - **Free Chat:** Discuss any topic in target language
  - **Correction Mode:** AI corrects every mistake
  - **Immersion Mode:** AI only responds in target language

### 4.2 Enhanced AI Capabilities
- Real-time grammar correction
- Vocabulary suggestions
- Cultural context explanations
- Pronunciation feedback (when using voice)
- Personalized learning recommendations
- Progress tracking in conversations
